Job Offers Web Scraping

Objective: Automate the extraction of job offers in Data Science from Bumeran for market analysis.

Description: A web scraper was built in Python using BeautifulSoup to collect job offers filtered by Data Science roles. It included extracting offer URLs and key details such as job title, description, district, and work modality. Data was cleaned and exported to CSV.

Tools: Python, BeautifulSoup, Pandas, Jupyter Notebook

Results: Structured dataset generated for analyzing geographic and remote/on-site work trends.

Repository: Bumeran DS Jobs Scraper

Codeforces Data Analysis

Objective: Analyze programming contest performance using data from the Codeforces API.

Description: Extracted contest, user, and submission data between July and December 2024. Data cleaning, transformation, and variable engineering (e.g., response time, rating achieved). Visualizations included histograms, boxplots, density plots, and linear regression.

Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Statsmodels

Results: In-depth analysis of programmer performance, revealing patterns by language and submission speed.

Repository: Codeforces API Project

School Access in Peru – Streamlit App

Objective: Visualize school accessibility in rural areas of Peru.

Description: Developed an interactive Streamlit app using GeoPandas and Folium. Created static and dynamic maps for schools (preschool, primary, secondary). Performed proximity analysis by district and influence zones in Huancavelica and Ayacucho.

Tools: Python, GeoPandas, Folium, Streamlit, OpenStreetMap

Results: A visual platform that highlights urban-rural disparities in school access.

Repository: High School Access – Peru

Forest Cover and Legal Protection in Peru

Objective: Assess the legal impact on forest cover in protected areas.

Description: Extracted polygons of protected areas (OSM Overpass API) and MODIS data. Created buffers (5–25 km) and implemented regression discontinuity to compare forest cover inside/outside protected zones.

Tools: Python, OSMnx, Rasterio, Rioxarray, GeoPandas, Statsmodels

Results: Statistical evidence of positive effects of legal protection on forest conservation.

Repository: Spatial Analysis of Peru Protected Areas
